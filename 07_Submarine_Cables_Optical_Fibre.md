# Submarine Cables (Optical Fibre Cables)

## Ye Hain Kya?

**Submarine Cables** samundar ke neeche bichhaayi gayi **optical fibre cables** hain, jo alag-alag continents/countries ke beech internet data carry karti hain.

> **Analogy**: Jaise roads cities ko connect karti hain zameen pe, waise hi ye cables countries ko samundar ke neeche se connect karti hain — ye "internet ki asli backbone" hain.

## Kyun Zaroori Hain?

- **99% se zyada international internet traffic** in cables se hi jaata hai (satellites se nahi!)
- Video calls, websites, cloud data — sab in cables se hi transfer hota hai jab data ek country se dusre country jaata hai

## Kaise Kaam Karti Hain?

- **Optical Fibre** ke andar light signals ke through data travel karta hai (bahut fast, minimal loss)
- Cables hazaaron kilometers lambi hoti hain, samundar ki tehh mein bichhayi jaati hain
- Beech-beech mein **repeaters** lagte hain jo signal ko boost karte hain (kyunki lambi distance mein signal weak ho jaata hai)

## Interesting Facts

| Fact | Detail |
|---|---|
| Total Cables | 500+ submarine cables duniya bhar mein active hain |
| Companies | Google, Meta, Amazon jaisi companies bhi apni cables banati hain |
| Damage Risk | Fishing boats, earthquakes, anchors se cables damage ho sakti hain |
| Backup | Multiple cables hoti hain taaki ek cable fail ho to traffic reroute ho sake |

## Viva One-Liner

> "Submarine cables samundar ke neeche laid optical fibre cables hain jo duniya bhar ke internet traffic ka bulk carry karti hain — satellites se bahut zyada."
