# Modem, Router

## Modem Kya Hai?

**Modem (Modulator-Demodulator)** ek device hai jo **digital signal** (computer ka data) ko **analog signal** mein convert karta hai (aur wapas) taaki data telephone lines/cable lines ke through travel kar sake.

> **Analogy**: Modem ek translator jaisa hai — computer ki "language" (digital) ko ISP ki cable ki "language" (analog) mein badalta hai, aur wapas bhi.

- ISP (Internet Service Provider) se connection modem ke through aata hai
- Ye internet ko tumhare ghar tak "laata" hai

## Router Kya Hai?

**Router** ek device hai jo data packets ko ek network se dusre network tak sahi raaste se bhejta hai — aur ghar/office ke multiple devices ko internet share karne deta hai.

> **Analogy**: Router ek traffic police jaisa hai jo decide karta hai data ka packet kaunse raaste se jaaye taaki fastest aur sahi destination tak pahunche.

- WiFi router ghar ke sab devices (phone, laptop) ko internet connection deta hai
- Ye IP addresses assign karta hai devices ko (via DHCP)

## Modem vs Router

| Modem | Router |
|---|---|
| ISP se connection leke aata hai | Us connection ko multiple devices mein baantata hai |
| Ek hi device connect ho sakta hai directly | Multiple devices (WiFi/LAN) connect ho sakte hain |
| Digital ↔ Analog conversion karta hai | Data packets ko route karta hai sahi destination tak |

> Aajkal zyada tar ghar mein **Modem + Router combo device** milta hai (ek hi box mein dono functionality).

## Viva One-Liners

- **Q: Modem ka kaam kya hai?**
 A: Digital signal ko analog mein convert karna (aur vice versa) taaki ISP line se data travel kar sake.
- **Q: Router ka kaam kya hai?**
 A: Data packets ko sahi network/device tak route karna, aur internet ko multiple devices mein share karna.
