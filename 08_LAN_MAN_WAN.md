# LAN, MAN, WAN

## Network Types (Based on Area Coverage)

| Type | Full Form | Range | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PAN** | Personal Area Network | Kuch meters | Bluetooth earphones se phone |
| **LAN** | Local Area Network | Ek building/campus | College lab, office network |
| **MAN** | Metropolitan Area Network | Ek city | City ka cable TV network |
| **WAN** | Wide Area Network | Country/World | Internet khud ek WAN hai |

> **Analogy**: PAN = tumhara personal bag, LAN = tumhare hostel ka network, MAN = pura Nagpur city connected, WAN = pura duniya connected.

## LAN (Local Area Network)

- Chhoti area cover karta hai (ek building ya campus)
- High speed, low cost
- Example: College lab ke computers switch se connected

## MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

- Ek city ya bade area ko cover karta hai
- LAN se bada, WAN se chhota
- Example: City-wide cable network, government offices connect karna ek city mein

## WAN (Wide Area Network)

- Sabse bada — countries/continents ko connect karta hai
- Slower compared to LAN (zyada distance)
- Example: Internet, ek company ke multiple city branches connected

## Comparison Table

| Feature | LAN | MAN | WAN |
|---|---|---|---|
| Area | Building/Campus | City | Country/World |
| Speed | Fastest | Medium | Slower (relative) |
| Ownership | Private (single org) | Multiple orgs/ISP | Multiple orgs/ISPs |
| Cost | Low | Medium | High |

## Viva One-Liner

> "LAN chhoti area cover karta hai (building), MAN city-level, aur WAN sabse bada — poori duniya (internet is best example)."
