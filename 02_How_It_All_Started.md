# How It All Started? (History of Networking)

## Pehle Kya Tha

Shuru mein computers **standalone** hote the — koi ek dusre se connected nahi tha. Data transfer karna hota tha to floppy disks/tapes physically le jaani padti thi.

## ARPANET (1969) — The Beginning

- US Department of Defense ne **ARPANET** banaya — duniya ka pehla packet-switched network.
- Goal tha: agar ek connection fail ho jaaye (jaise war ke time), to data doosre raaste se pahunch jaaye.
- Sirf 4 universities connected thi shuru mein.

> **Analogy**: Pehle jaise dak (post) system tha — chitthi ek fixed route se jaati thi. ARPANET ne idea diya ki data ko chhote packets mein todo aur multiple raaston se bhejo — agar ek raasta band ho, dusre se pahunch jaaye.

## Packet Switching Ka Idea

Data ko poora ek saath nahi bheja jaata — chhote **packets** mein todke bheja jaata hai, jo independently travel karke destination pe reassemble hote hain.

## TCP/IP Ka Development (1970s-80s)

- Vint Cerf aur Bob Kahn ne **TCP/IP protocol** banaya — jo aaj bhi internet ki backbone hai.
- 1983 mein ARPANET ne officially TCP/IP adopt kiya — isi din ko "Internet ka birthday" mana jaata hai.

## World Wide Web (1989-91)

- Tim Berners-Lee ne **WWW (World Wide Web)** banaya — jisme HTTP, HTML, aur URLs introduce hue.
- Isse pehle internet tha, lekin **web pages/browsing** ka concept nahi tha — WWW ne internet ko "usable" bana diya common logo ke liye.

## Key Timeline Table

| Year | Event |
|---|---|
| 1969 | ARPANET launch hota hai |
| 1974 | TCP/IP concept propose hota hai |
| 1983 | ARPANET TCP/IP adopt karta hai |
| 1989 | Tim Berners-Lee WWW invent karte hain |
| 1990s | Internet public ke liye available hota hai |

## Viva One-Liner

> "Internet ARPANET se start hua tha, packet switching ka use karke, aur TCP/IP protocol ne isse standardize kiya."
